Questions? Email or tweet me. This project is open source.

You can also donate through or to help me pay my Google BigQuery bill.

Please note that the IP ranges used are best effort attempts at identifying networks owned by governments and industries. They may be inaccurate or incomplete, and may have changed hands over time (although so far we have seen no examples of this). Any controversial edits you find should be independently verified.

The IP ranges are maintained by the Twitter account owners, please write them (or me) if you have corrections.
